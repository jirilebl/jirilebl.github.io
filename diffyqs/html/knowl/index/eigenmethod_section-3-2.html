Let \(A\) be a constant square matrix. Suppose \(\lambda\) is a scalar and \(\vec{v}\) is a nonzero vector such that
\begin{equation} A \vec{v} = \lambda \vec{v}. \end{equation}
We call such a \(\lambda\) an eigenvalue of \(A\text{,}\) and we call \(\vec{v}\) a corresponding eigenvector.
