Let us find a particular solution. There can be no conflicts when trying to solve for the undetermined coefficients by trying \(x_p = A \cos (\omega t) + B \sin (\omega t)\text{.}\) We plug in and solve for \(A\) and \(B\text{.}\) We get (the tedious details are left to reader)
\begin{equation} \bigl((\omega_0^2 - \omega^2)B - 2\omega p A\bigr) \sin (\omega t) + \bigl((\omega_0^2 - \omega^2)A + 2\omega p B\bigr) \cos (\omega t) = \frac{F_0}{m} \cos (\omega t) . \end{equation}
