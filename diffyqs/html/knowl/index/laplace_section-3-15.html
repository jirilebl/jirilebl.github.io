Theorem 6.1.1. Linearity of the Laplace transform.

If \(A\text{,}\) \(B\text{,}\) and \(C\) are constants, then
\begin{equation} \mybxbg{~~ \mathcal{L} \bigl\{ A f(t) + B g(t) \bigr\} = A \mathcal{L} \bigl\{ f(t) \bigr\} + B \mathcal{L} \bigl\{ g(t) \bigr\} , ~~} \end{equation}
and in particular,
\begin{equation} \mathcal{L} \bigl\{ C f(t) \bigr\} = C \mathcal{L} \bigl\{ f(t) \bigr\} . \end{equation}
