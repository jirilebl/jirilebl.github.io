The characteristic equation is \(r^2 + k^2 = 0\text{,}\) and the roots are \(r = \pm ik\text{.}\) By the theorem, the general solution is
\begin{equation} y = C_1 \cos (kx) + C_2 \sin (kx) . \end{equation}
