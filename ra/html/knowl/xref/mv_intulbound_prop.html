Proposition 10.1.6.

Let \(R \subset \R^n\) be a closed rectangle and \(f \colon R \to \R\) a bounded function. Let \(m, M \in \R\) be such that for all \(x \in R\text{,}\) we have \(m \leq f(x) \leq M\text{.}\) Then
\begin{equation} m \, V(R) \leq \underline{\int_R} f \leq \overline{\int_R} f \leq M \, V(R).\tag{10.1} \end{equation}
