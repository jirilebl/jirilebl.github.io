Exercise 10.1.4.

Suppose \(R \subset \R^n\) is a closed rectangle with the length of one of the sides equal to 0, and suppose \(S\) is a closed rectangle with \(R \subset S\text{.}\) If \(f \colon S \to \R\) is a bounded function such that \(f(x) = 0\) for \(x \in S \setminus R\text{,}\) show that \(f \in \sR(S)\) and \(\int_S f = 0\text{.}\)
