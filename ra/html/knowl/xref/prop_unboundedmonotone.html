Proposition 2.3.10.

Suppose \(\{ x_n \}_{n=1}^\infty\) is a monotone unbounded sequence. Then
\begin{equation} \lim_{n \to \infty} x_n = \begin{cases} \infty & \text{if } \{ x_n \}_{n=1}^\infty \text{ is increasing,} \\ -\infty & \text{if } \{ x_n \}_{n=1}^\infty \text{ is decreasing.} \end{cases} \end{equation}
