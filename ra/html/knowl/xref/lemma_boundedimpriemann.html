Lemma 5.2.8.

Let \(f \colon [a,b] \to \R\) be a bounded function, \(\{ a_n \}_{n=1}^\infty\) and \(\{b_n \}_{n=1}^\infty\) be sequences such that \(a < a_n < b_n < b\) for all \(n\text{,}\) with \(\lim_{n\to\infty} a_n = a\) and \(\lim_{n\to\infty} b_n = b\text{.}\) Suppose \(f \in \sR\bigl([a_n,b_n]\bigr)\) for all \(n\text{.}\) Then \(f \in \sR\bigl([a,b]\bigr)\) and
\begin{equation} \int_a^b f = \lim_{n \to \infty} \int_{a_n}^{b_n} f . \end{equation}
