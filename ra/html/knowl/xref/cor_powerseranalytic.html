Corollary 11.3.7.

For every \(a \in \C\text{,}\) if \(\sum_{k=0}^\infty c_k {(z-a)}^k\) converges to \(f(z)\) in \(B(a,\rho)\) and \(b \in B(a,\rho)\text{,}\) then there exists a power series \(\sum_{k=0}^\infty d_k {(z-b)}^k\) that converges to \(f(z)\) in \(B(b,\rho-\sabs{b-a})\text{.}\)
