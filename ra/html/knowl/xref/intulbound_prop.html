Proposition 5.1.8.

Let \(f \colon [a,b] \to \R\) be a bounded function. Let \(m, M \in \R\) be such that for all \(x \in [a,b]\text{,}\) we have \(m \leq f(x) \leq M\text{.}\) Then
\begin{equation} m(b-a) \leq \underline{\int_a^b} f \leq \overline{\int_a^b} f \leq M(b-a) .\tag{5.2} \end{equation}
