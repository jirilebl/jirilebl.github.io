Exercise 1.3.4.

Finish the proof of Proposition 1.3.7. That is, given a set \(D\text{,}\) and two bounded functions \(f \colon D \to \R\) and \(g \colon D \to \R\) such that \(f(x) \leq g(x)\) for all \(x \in D\text{,}\) prove that
\begin{equation} \inf_{x\in D} f(x) \leq \inf_{x\in D} g(x) . \end{equation}
