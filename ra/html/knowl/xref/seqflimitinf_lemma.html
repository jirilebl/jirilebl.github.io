Lemma 3.5.5.

Suppose \(f \colon S \to \R\) is a function, \(\infty\) is a cluster point of \(S \subset \R\text{,}\) and \(L \in \R\text{.}\) Then
\begin{equation} \lim_{x\to\infty} f(x) = L \qquad \text{if and only if} \qquad \lim_{n\to\infty} f(x_n) = L \end{equation}
for all sequences \(\{ x_n \}_{n=1}^\infty\) in \(S\) such that \(\lim\limits_{n\to\infty} x_n = \infty\text{.}\)
