Exercise 7.3.10.

Take \(\R^* = \{ -\infty \} \cup \R \cup \{ \infty \}\) be the extended reals. Define \(d(x,y) \coloneqq \babs{ \frac{x}{1+\sabs{x}} - \frac{y}{1+\sabs{y}} }\) if \(x, y \in \R\text{,}\) define \(d(\infty,x) \coloneqq \babs{ 1 - \frac{x}{1+\sabs{x}} }\text{,}\) \(d(-\infty,x) \coloneqq \babs{ 1 + \frac{x}{1+\sabs{x}} }\) for all \(x \in \R\text{,}\) and let \(d(\infty,-\infty) \coloneqq 2\text{.}\)

  1. Show that \((\R^*,d)\) is a metric space.
