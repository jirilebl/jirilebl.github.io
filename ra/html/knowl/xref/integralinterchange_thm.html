Theorem 6.2.4.

Let \(\{ f_n \}_{n=1}^\infty\) be a sequence of Riemann integrable functions \(f_n \colon [a,b] \to \R\) converging uniformly to \(f \colon [a,b] \to \R\text{.}\) Then \(f\) is Riemann integrable, and
\begin{equation} \int_a^b f = \lim_{n\to\infty} \int_a^b f_n . \end{equation}
