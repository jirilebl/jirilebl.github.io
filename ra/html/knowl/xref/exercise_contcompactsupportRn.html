Exercise 10.1.1.

Suppose \(U \subset \R^n\) is open and \(f \colon U \to \R\) is continuous and of compact support. Show that the function \(\widetilde{f} \colon \R^n \to \R\text{,}\) where
\begin{equation} \widetilde{f}(x) \coloneqq \begin{cases} f(x) & \text{if } x \in U, \\ 0 & \text{otherwise,} \end{cases} \end{equation}
is continuous.
