Proposition 8.4.2.

Let \(U \subset \R^n\) be a convex open set, \(f \colon U \to \R^m\) be a differentiable function, and \(M\) be such that
\begin{equation} \bnorm{f'(p)} \leq M \qquad \text{for all } p \in U. \end{equation}
Then \(f\) is Lipschitz with constant \(M\text{,}\) that is,
\begin{equation} \bnorm{f(p)-f(q)} \leq M \snorm{p-q} \qquad \text{for all } p,q \in U. \end{equation}
