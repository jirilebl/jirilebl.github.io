Proposition 10.1.10. Linearity.

Let \(R \subset \R^n\) be a closed rectangle and let \(f\) and \(g\) be in \(\sR(R)\) and \(\alpha \in \R\text{.}\)
🔗
  1. \(\alpha f\) is in \(\sR(R)\) and
    \begin{equation} \int_R \alpha f = \alpha \int_R f . \end{equation}
    🔗
    🔗
  2. \(f+g\) is in \(\sR(R)\) and
    \begin{equation} \int_R (f+g) = \int_R f + \int_R g . \end{equation}
    🔗
    🔗
