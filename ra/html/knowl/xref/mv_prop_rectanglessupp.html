Proposition 10.1.19.

Suppose \(f \colon \R^n \to \R\) is a continuous function with compact support. If \(R\) and \(S\) are closed rectangles such that \(\operatorname{supp}(f) \subset R\) and \(\operatorname{supp}(f) \subset S\text{,}\) then
\begin{equation} \int_S f = \int_R f . \end{equation}
