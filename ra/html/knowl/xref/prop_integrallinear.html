Proposition 5.2.4. Linearity.

Let \(f\) and \(g\) be in \(\sR\bigl([a,b]\bigr)\) and \(\alpha \in \R\text{.}\)
🔗
  1. \(\alpha f\) is in \(\sR\bigl([a,b]\bigr)\) and
    \begin{equation} \int_a^b \alpha f(x) \,dx = \alpha \int_a^b f(x) \,dx . \end{equation}
    🔗
    🔗
  2. \(f+g\) is in \(\sR\bigl([a,b]\bigr)\) and
    \begin{equation} \int_a^b \bigl( f(x)+g(x) \bigr) \,dx = \int_a^b f(x) \,dx + \int_a^b g(x) \,dx . \end{equation}

    🔗
