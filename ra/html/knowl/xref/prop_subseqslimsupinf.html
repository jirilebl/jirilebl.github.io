Proposition 2.3.6.

Suppose \(\{ x_n \}_{n=1}^\infty\) is a bounded sequence and \(\{ x_{n_k} \}_{k=1}^\infty\) is a subsequence. Then
\begin{equation} \liminf_{n\to\infty} x_n \leq \liminf_{k\to\infty} x_{n_k} \leq \limsup_{k\to\infty} x_{n_k} \leq \limsup_{n\to\infty} x_n . \end{equation}
