Lemma 11.5.1.

Let \(\epsilon > 0\text{,}\) let \(p(z)\) be a nonconstant complex polynomial, or more generally a nonconstant power series converging in \(B(z_0,\epsilon)\text{,}\) and suppose \(p(z_0) \neq 0\text{.}\) Then there exists a \(w \in B(z_0,\epsilon)\) such that \(\babs{p(w)} < \babs{p(z_0)}\text{.}\)
