Proposition 10.1.12.

Let \(R \subset \R^n\) be a closed rectangle and \(f \colon R \to \R\) a bounded function. Then \(f \in \sR(R)\) if and only if for every \(\epsilon > 0\text{,}\) there exists a partition \(P\) of \(R\) such that
\begin{equation} U(P,f) - L(P,f) < \epsilon . \end{equation}
