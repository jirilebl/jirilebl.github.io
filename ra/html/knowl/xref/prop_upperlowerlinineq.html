Proposition 5.2.5.

Let \(f \colon [a,b] \to \R\) and \(g \colon [a,b] \to \R\) be bounded functions. Then
\begin{equation} \overline{\int_a^b} (f+g) \leq \overline{\int_a^b}f+\overline{\int_a^b}g , \qquad \text{and} \qquad \underline{\int_a^b} (f+g) \geq \underline{\int_a^b}f+\underline{\int_a^b}g . \end{equation}
