Lemma 5.2.1.

Suppose \(a < b < c\) and \(f \colon [a,c] \to \R\) is a bounded function. Then
\begin{equation} \underline{\int_a^c} f = \underline{\int_a^b} f + \underline{\int_b^c} f \quad \text{and} \quad \overline{\int_a^c} f = \overline{\int_a^b} f + \overline{\int_b^c} f . \end{equation}
