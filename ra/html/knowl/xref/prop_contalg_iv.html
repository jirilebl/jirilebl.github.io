Item iv.
If \(\lim_{n\to\infty} y_n \neq 0\) and \(y_n \neq 0\) for all \(n \in \N\text{,}\) then the sequence \(\{ z_n \}_{n=1}^\infty\text{,}\) where \(z_n \coloneqq \dfrac{x_n}{y_n}\text{,}\) converges and
\begin{equation} \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{x_n}{y_n} = \lim_{n \to \infty} z_n = \frac{\lim_{n \to \infty} x_n}{\lim_{n \to \infty} y_n} . \end{equation}
