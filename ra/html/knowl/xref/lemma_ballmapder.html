Lemma 10.3.9.

Suppose \(U \subset \R^n\) is an open set, \(B \subset U\) is an open (resp. closed) ball of radius at most \(r\text{,}\) \(f \colon U \to \R^n\) is continuously differentiable, and suppose \(\bnorm{f'(x)} \leq M\) for all \(x \in B\text{.}\) Then \(f(B) \subset B'\text{,}\) where \(B'\) is an open (resp. closed) ball of radius at most \(Mr\text{.}\)
