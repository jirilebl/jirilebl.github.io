Proposition 8.1.16.

Let \(X\text{,}\) \(Y\text{,}\) and \(Z\) be vector spaces.
🔗
  1. If \(A \in L(X,Y)\text{,}\) then \(A0 = 0\text{.}\)
    🔗
    🔗
  2. If \(A,B \in L(X,Y)\text{,}\) then \(A+B \in L(X,Y)\text{.}\)
    🔗
    🔗
  3. If \(A \in L(X,Y)\) and \(a \in \R\text{,}\) then \(aA \in L(X,Y)\text{.}\)
    🔗
    🔗
  4. If \(A \in L(Y,Z)\) and \(B \in L(X,Y)\text{,}\) then \(AB \in L(X,Z)\text{.}\)
    🔗
    🔗
  5. If \(A \in L(X,Y)\) is invertible, then \(A^{-1} \in L(Y,X)\text{.}\)
    🔗
    🔗
