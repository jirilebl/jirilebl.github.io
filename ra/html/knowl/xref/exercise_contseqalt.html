Exercise 3.2.13.

Let \(f \colon S \to \R\) be a function and \(c \in S\) such that for every sequence \(\{ x_n \}_{n=1}^\infty\) in \(S\) with \(\lim_{n\to\infty} x_n = c\text{,}\) the sequence \(\bigl\{ f(x_n) \bigr\}_{n=1}^\infty\) converges. Show that \(f\) is continuous at \(c\text{.}\)
