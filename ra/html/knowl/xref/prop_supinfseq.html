Proposition 2.1.13.

Let \(S \subset \R\) be a nonempty bounded set. Then there exist monotone sequences \(\{ x_n \}_{n=1}^\infty\) and \(\{ y_n \}_{n=1}^\infty\) such that \(x_n, y_n \in S\) and
\begin{equation} \sup\,S = \lim_{n\to \infty} x_n \qquad \text{and} \qquad \inf\,S = \lim_{n\to\infty} y_n . \end{equation}
