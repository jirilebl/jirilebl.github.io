Exercise 1.3.7.

Let \(D\) be a nonempty set. Suppose \(f \colon D \to \R\) and \(g \colon D \to \R\) are bounded functions.
🔗
  1. Show
    \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} & \sup_{x\in D} \bigl(f(x) + g(x) \bigr) \leq \sup_{x\in D} f(x) + \sup_{x\in D} g(x) \qquad \text{and} \\ & \inf_{x\in D} \bigl(f(x) + g(x) \bigr) \geq \inf_{x\in D} f(x) + \inf_{x\in D} g(x) . \end{aligned} \end{equation}
    🔗
    🔗
  2. Find an example (or examples) where we obtain strict inequalities.
