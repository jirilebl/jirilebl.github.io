Exercise 7.1.6.

Let \((X,d_X)\) and \((Y,d_Y)\) be metric spaces.
🔗
  1. Show that \((X \times Y,d)\) with \(d\bigl( (x_1,y_1), (x_2,y_2) \bigr) \coloneqq d_X(x_1,x_2) + d_Y(y_1,y_2)\) is a metric space.
    🔗
    🔗
  2. Show that \((X \times Y,d)\) with \(d\bigl( (x_1,y_1), (x_2,y_2) \bigr) \coloneqq \max \bigl\{ d_X(x_1,x_2) , d_Y(y_1,y_2) \bigr\}\) is a metric space.
