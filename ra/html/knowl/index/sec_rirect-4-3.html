Definition 10.1.3.

Let \(f \colon R \to \R\) be a bounded function on a closed rectangle \(R \subset \R^n\text{.}\) Define
\begin{equation} \begin{aligned} & \underline{\int_R} f \coloneqq \sup \, \bigl\{ L(P,f) : P \text{ a partition of } R \bigr\} , \\ & \overline{\int_R} f \coloneqq \inf \, \bigl\{ U(P,f) : P \text{ a partition of } R \bigr\} . \end{aligned} \end{equation}
We call \(\underline{\int}\) the lower Darboux integral and \(\overline{\int}\) the upper Darboux integral.
