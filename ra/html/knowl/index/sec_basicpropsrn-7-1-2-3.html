Item iii.

If there exists an upper bound \(b_0\) of \(E\) such that \(b_0 \leq b\) for all upper bounds \(b\) of \(E\text{,}\) then \(b_0\) is called the least upper bound or the supremum of \(E\text{.}\) See Figure 1.1. We write
\begin{equation} \sup\, E \coloneqq b_0 . \end{equation}
