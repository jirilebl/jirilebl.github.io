Theorem 5.3.5. Change of variables.

Let \(g \colon [a,b] \to \R\) be a continuously differentiable function, let \(f \colon [c,d] \to \R\) be continuous, and suppose \(g\bigl([a,b]\bigr) \subset [c,d]\text{.}\) Then
\begin{equation} \int_a^b f\bigl(g(x)\bigr)\, g'(x)\, dx = \int_{g(a)}^{g(b)} f(u)\, du . \end{equation}
