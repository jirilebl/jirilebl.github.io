Definition 2.1.16.

Let \(\{ x_n \}_{n=1}^\infty\) be a sequence. Let \(\{ n_i \}_{i=1}^\infty\) be a strictly increasing sequence of natural numbers, that is, \(n_i < n_{i+1}\) for all \(i \in \N\) (in other words \(n_1 < n_2 < n_3 < \cdots\)). The sequence
\begin{equation} \{ x_{n_i} \}_{i=1}^\infty \end{equation}
is called a subsequence of \(\{ x_n \}_{n=1}^\infty\text{.}\)
