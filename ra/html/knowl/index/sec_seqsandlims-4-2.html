Definition 2.1.14.

For a sequence \(\{ x_n \}_{n=1}^\infty\text{,}\) the \(K\)-tail (where \(K \in \N\)), or just the tail, of \(\{ x_n \}_{n=1}^\infty\) is the sequence starting at \(K+1\text{,}\) usually written as
\begin{equation} \{ x_{n+K} \}_{n=1}^\infty \qquad \text{or} \qquad \{ x_n \}_{n=K+1}^\infty . \end{equation}
