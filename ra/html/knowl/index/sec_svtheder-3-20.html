Theorem 8.3.7. Chain rule.

Let \(U \subset \R^n\) and \(V \subset \R^m\) be open sets, \(f \colon U \to \R^m\) be differentiable at \(p \in U\text{,}\) \(f(U) \subset V\text{,}\) and let \(g \colon V \to \R^\ell\) be differentiable at \(f(p)\text{.}\) Then \(F \colon U \to \R^{\ell}\) defined by
\begin{equation} F(x) \coloneqq g\bigl(f(x)\bigr) \end{equation}
is differentiable at \(p\text{,}\) and
\begin{equation} F'(p) = g'\bigl(f(p)\bigr) f'(p) . \end{equation}
