A concept we will encounter over and over is the absolute value. You want to think of the absolute value as the “size” of a real number. Here is the formal definition:
\begin{equation} \sabs{x} \coloneqq \begin{cases} x & \text{if } x \geq 0, \\ -x & \text{if } x < 0 . \end{cases} \end{equation}
