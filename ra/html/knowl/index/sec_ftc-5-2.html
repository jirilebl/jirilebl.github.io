A theorem often used in calculus to solve integrals is the change of variables theorem, you may have called it \(u\)-substitution. To make it easy to connect to your calculus class, we will also call the new variable \(u\text{,}\) that is, we make the substitution \(u=g(x)\text{.}\) Recall a function is continuously differentiable if it is differentiable and the derivative is continuous.
