Exercise 2.5.15.

(Challenging)   Suppose \(\{ x_n\}_{n=1}^\infty\) is a decreasing sequence of positive numbers. The proof of convergence/divergence for the \(p\)-series generalizes. Prove the Cauchy condensation principle:
\begin{equation} \sum_{n=1}^\infty x_n \qquad \text{converges if and only if} \qquad \sum_{n=1}^\infty 2^n x_{2^n} \qquad \text{converges}. \end{equation}
