Item 11.7.7.b.
Show that functions of the form
\begin{equation} c_0 + \sum_{k=1}^n c_k \, z^k + \sum_{k=1}^n c_{-k}\, \bar{z}^k \end{equation}
are dense in \(C(S^1,\C)\text{.}\) These functions are called harmonic polynomials, and this approximation leads to, for example, the solution of the steady state heat problem.
